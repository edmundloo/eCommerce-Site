Description

Though several years out of production the Yashica Mat-124G is still one of the most widely used TLRs around. The reasons for its popularity are simple: Quality, affordability and ease of use. A favorite of photographers getting started in medium format, it's dependability and features such as 120/220 capability, bright fresnel screen, built-in 3x magnifier and sports-finder frame also make it a mainstay in many a pro's working lineup.
